How to Use:

  • Click "Start Camera" to begin (you'll need to allow camera access)
  • Position your face in the frame so it's clearly visible
  • Select different glasses styles to see how they look on you
  • Use the sliders to fine-tune the glasses fit and position
  • Move your head to see how the glasses adjust with your movement
  • Click "Reset Adjustments" if you need to return to default settings
  • Click "Stop Camera" when you're finished
